  • ESG and Net Zero commitments require operational validation, not design intent

Our Solution

Standards-compliant (WHO/WELL/LEED), deterministic, AI-assisted environmental control platform

ClimaNexus introduces a new layer: A control intelligence that sits above HVAC devices and orchestrates the environment.

01

IAQ-Driven Operation

Ventilation and system behavior respond directly to CO₂, PM2.5, Temperature, and Humidity.

02

Deterministic Control Logic

Clearly defined states: NORMAL / IAQ_BOOST / HEAT_ASSIST / DEHUM / ALARM. Priority-based execution ensures stable operation.

03

Safety-First Architecture

Heating and dehumidification only activate when airflow is verified. Prevents unsafe or inefficient system states.

04

AI-Assisted Optimization

AI improves performance parameters. But cannot override safety or core control logic.

05

Edge-First Reliability

System runs independently of cloud. Ensures stability and fail-safe operation.

06

Multi-System Integration

Compatible with diverse HVAC configurations. Supports scalable deployment across projects.
